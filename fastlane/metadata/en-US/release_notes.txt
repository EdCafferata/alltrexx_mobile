Version 2.2.0

New
• OWM colour legend — a colour scale appears in the map corner for each weather layer so you instantly understand what you see
• Wind speed in knots — displayed as a number below the Beaufort circle on the map
• Precipitation mm/h — current rainfall intensity added to the info bar
• Info button moved to the top row, next to the share button

Live weather data
Wind direction, Beaufort force, temperature, visibility, air pressure and wave height — shown directly on screen via Open-Meteo.

OpenWeatherMap layers
Precipitation, clouds, wind, pressure or temperature as an overlay on your navigation map. Free OWM account required.

Markermeer water level
Automatically retrieved via Rijkswaterstaat, updated every 10 minutes (cm NAP).

GPS battery saving
Four automatic GPS profiles based on sailing speed — up to 80% less battery use when stationary.

Course-up mode
The map automatically rotates to match your heading.

Apple Watch
Record GPX tracks independently, add waypoints and wirelessly send the track to your iPhone.
